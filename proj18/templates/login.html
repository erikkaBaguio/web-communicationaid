a communication-aide for non-verbal kids

Pic-A-Talk is a communication-aide specifically made for children with special needs. A less-hassle alternative to communicating with your special one! It's easy to use! Coose a category, click an image and an audio plays the image's name. It's like a translator! It's also a good learning experience since they are exposed to the image and the word, helping them identify things. Parents can also use this app to monitor their child's activity, share stories about their child with other parents and contact the right teachers and therapists. Sign up now!
